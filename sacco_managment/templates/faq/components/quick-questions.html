Frequently Asked Questions

To join our SACCO, visit any of our branches with your national ID, passport photo, and minimum share capital. You'll need to fill out a membership application form and attend a brief orientation session.

Members enjoy competitive dividend payments, access to affordable loans, financial education, and voting rights in the SACCO's annual general meetings. We also offer insurance products and investment opportunities.

After being a member for at least 3 months and making regular savings, you can apply for a loan through our mobile app, website, or at any branch. Loan approval depends on your savings history and credit assessment.

Our interest rates range from 12-18% per annum depending on loan type and duration. We offer lower rates than commercial banks because we're member-owned and not-for-profit.

Account Management

Download our mobile app or visit our website, then register using your membership number. You'll receive an OTP to verify your identity and set up login credentials.

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered phone number or email. You'll receive a reset link. Alternatively, visit any branch with your ID for assistance.

You can update contact information through the app or website. For name or ID changes, please visit a branch with supporting documents (marriage certificate, deed poll, etc.).

Transactions

Internal transfers between SACCO accounts are instant. Bank transfers take 1-2 business days. Mobile money transfers are usually completed within minutes.

Daily limits: 5,000,000 UGX via mobile, 50,000,000 UGX at branches. Higher limits can be arranged for verified business accounts with prior notice.

Immediately call our 24/7 fraud hotline at 0800 200 300 or email security@sacco.co.ug. Freeze your account via the app if needed while we investigate.

Pending transactions can be cancelled in your app. Completed transactions may be reversible within 30 minutes if recipient hasn't withdrawn funds. Contact support immediately.

Security

We're regulated by the Uganda Cooperative Alliance and insured up to 50,000,000 UGX per member. Funds are kept in tier-1 banks and we use military-grade encryption.

The app features biometric login, transaction PIN, automatic logout, device binding, and real-time alerts. We never store full card numbers or passwords.

Immediately call us to freeze your account or use another device to log in and remotely wipe the app. Your funds remain safe as transactions require additional authentication.

We never ask for passwords via email/SMS. Official communications will always address you by name and use @sacco.co.ug domains. When in doubt, call us directly.

Accounts with no transactions for 12 months incur a 5,000 UGX monthly dormancy fee. Reactivate by making a deposit at any branch.
